Vicari Human Striatium (VHS)

Dataset summary

  • Original publication DOI: https://doi.org/10.1101/2023.01.26.525195

  • Specific data link: https://figshare.scilifelab.se/articles/dataset/Spatial_Multimodal_Analysis_SMA_-_Spatial_Transcriptomics/22778920

  • Original abstract: We present a spatial omics approach that merges and expands the capabilities of independently performed in situ assays on a single tissue section. Our spatial multimodal analysis combines histology, mass spectrometry imaging, and spatial transcriptomics to facilitate precise measurements of mRNA transcripts and low-molecular weight metabolites across tissue regions. We demonstrate the potential of our method using murine and human brain samples in the context of dopamine and Parkinson’s disease.
