Abalo Human Squamous Cell Carcinoma (AHSCC)

Dataset summary

  • Original publication DOI: https://doi.org/10.17632/2bh5fchcv6.1

  • Specific data link: https://data.mendeley.com/datasets/2bh5fchcv6/1

  • Original abstract: Visium experiments on a squamous cell carcinoma from a human donor. Four serial 10 µm sections spaced 150 µm apart. Processed sequencing data from 10X Genomics Space Ranger version 1.0.0. A skin biopsy was collected from a patient with suspected squamous cell carcinoma after obtaining informed consent. The diagnosis was histologically verified by a board-certified dermatopathologist. The study protocol was approved by the Institutional Review Board at Stanford University Medical Center (protocol #21750).
